Numbers

Numbers include integers (int) and real or floating point (float).

  • Examples: 24, 3.5, -1027, 3.14159
  • Operators: +, -, *, /, // (floor div), % (modulo), **
  • Comparison: ==, !=, >, <, >=, <=
