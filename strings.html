Strings

Python does not have a type for characters, instead it supports strings of characters surrounded by single quotes (') or double quotes (").

  • Examples: 'Vienna', 'Hello world!', "The quick brown FOX"
  • Operators: + (concatenate), * (repeat), slices (substrings)
  • Comparison: ==, !=, >, <, >=, <=
