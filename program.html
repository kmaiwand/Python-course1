Why program?

To give a computer specific instructions to perform a task. Instructions must be formulated such that a computer can understand them.

But most importantly - programming is fun! As John Guttag put it, "Programming is the most fun you can have with your clothes on."

Why Python?

Python is a powerful programming language, yet relatively easy to use.

  • Easy to learn - friendly syntax
  • Widely used - has a large and dedicated community that develops tools for and around the language
  • Easy to debug - it is interpreted, and comes with an environment

In this class, we will probably just scratch the surface, but learn enough to write small/simple programs.
