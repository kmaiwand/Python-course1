How to use this template:

1. add a new slide:
	Copy an existing slide into new.html
	In this new file, leave the header and fooret alone
	Feel free to change all else that you want
	Open Assets/slides.js
		Add the list containing "[ the name, the title]" in the appropriate position in slides
		
2. Move an existing slide around:
	Open Assets/slides.js
		Move the list containing [name, title] of the slide to the appropriate position in slides

3. Change the look and feel in tutorial.css
